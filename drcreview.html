'DR. CEURACLE' Cica Regen Vegan Sunscreen 50+++ Review

Reviewed by Leigh NOV 2022

Verdict:

Long ago I proclaimed myself Queen of the sunscreen minions. I am OBSESSED guys. So I was super excited to try Dr. Ceuracles Cica Regen Vegan 50+++ Sunscreen and I had high hopes for the below reasons. The brand claims it features a patented Cica Barrier Complex to soothe and strengthen the skin barrier whilst also combining essence, moisturizer, and sun protection steps in one. This. Made. Me. So. Happy! I could feel the time saved in my morning routine already.

About the brand

“The Cosmeceutical Pioneer in Korea” is Dr Ceuracle’s slogan. The brand uses clinical expertise to develop top-level cosmeceutical lines and focuses on curing, balancing and caring for the skin by creating enhanced skincare solutions for every person and every skin type. They follow 3 distinct categories: Natural Derma, the signature line of Dr Ceuracle’s “botanic science” theory; Medical Derma, advanced formulas that are proven to have significant effect on specific skin concerns; and Makeup, l ight products to provide a natural glow and healthy-looking skin.

How to use

  • Apply to face and neck at the end of your skincare routine
  • Reapply every 2-3 hours depending on level of sun exposure

First Impressions

The sunscreen has a light cream texture, spreads easily and sinks into the skin nicely. I ended up with a semi-matte finish- so not dewy but not dry. The white cast that some sunscreens leave was not present, yay for that! Initially, there is a very noticeable herbal scent. I'm assuming it is rosemary oil which I personally love however for those who are more sensitive to smells it doesn’t linger. I applied Dr.Ceuracle Cica Regen Vegan Sun all over my face and neck.

YES YES YES guys, I love this product. The finish is perfect for me, I like a matte look. The formula is sweat and water resistant and re-applies nicely. Touching on the Rosemary scent, I enjoy this however I know some of you guys don't enjoy fragrance. As I said it does not linger but it could be offputting initially. I still give it four crowns!
