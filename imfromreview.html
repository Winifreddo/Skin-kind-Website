'I’m From' Beet Purifying Mask Review

Reviewed by Leigh DEC 2022

Verdict:

I enjoy a good wash-off mask, and I'm From's products have traditionally given my skin great results. The I'm From Beet Purifying Mask makes the claim that it will reduce the appearance of pores, manage sebum, and give users a clear complexion. These are fairly typical claims for clay masks, however the I'm From Beet is special since it contains 1.5% red beet enzyme from Damyang, Korea. After the mask is removed, this enzyme can help the skin retain moisture so that it feels hydrated rather than tight.

About the brand

A simple slogan — "Nature as it as, it's I'm From" — encapsulates the true essence behind K Beauty brand I'm From. The brand takes inspiration from nature, and are transparent with their ingredients inclusive of where ingredients have been harvested. Popular ingredients among the brand are rice, honey, ginseng and mugwort that work to combat several skin problems. They use safe, effective formulas and no harmful additives. They have been the masterminds behind some of the most successful products on the K beauty market such as the 'I'm from' rice toner which is created with organic and pesticide-free rice, this toner gently moisturises and brightens skin, while also removing impurities and excess oils at the same time.

How to use

  • Apply to a cleansed face
  • Wait 10-15 minutes (but do not let the mask dry completely)
  • rinse off

First Impressions

The mask is extremely creamy and simple to use. It doesn't irritate or dry my skin. There is a faint fruity scent to it however it is just that.. faint and the smell doesn't linger. The mask stayed moist the whole duration it was on my face.

This mask did not disappoint and whenever I feel like my skin could use a little extra purifying you will find me reaching for it. I used the I’m From Beet Mask all over the face, even on the dry areas (weep, I have a lot), and got lovely results. My skin felt softer and smoother instantly, and my dry areas were left feeling stun stun stunning. For me, this is a good mask for keeping the pores looking tight.
