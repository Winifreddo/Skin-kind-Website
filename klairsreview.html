'KLAIRS' Freshly Juiced Vitamin E mask Review

Reviewed by Leigh DEC 2022

Verdict:

For me KLAIRS can really pull it out of the bag on some products and really suck with others (my opinion guys). I love their unscented hydration toner however was really disappointed with their Vitamin C essence. So I was a little less enthused to try the Vitamin E mask. The Klairs Freshly Juiced Vitamin E Mask promises to brighten, improve the look of wrinkles, and prevent other signs of aging. It is cruelty free, vegan friendly , and made without artificial fragrances, silicon oils, and colorants.

About the brand

Klairs are a skincare brand that was founded in 2010 in Seoul. They focus on developing functional products meant for sensitive skin. It is a cruelty-free, vegan, and eco-friendly brand. their philosophy is to never use harsh ingredients, never test on animals and offer high quality at reasonable prices. They are certified vegan.

How to use

  • Apply to a cleansed face and use at the end of your skincare routine.
  • It can be used as either moisturiser or a mask
  • I used it as a night mask

First Impressions

Lets get into it! I’ve been using Klairs Freshly Juiced Vitamin E Mask three times a week in my evening routine. I use the tiniest amount as it really goes a long way so definitely use less than you think you’ll need - use to much and you might experience stickiness, I did the first time :(. One thing I was amused by was the consistency. It has a custard like consistency and after use the finger indents disappear and it goes back to looking brand new. So each time you use it it feels like you are dipping into a brand new jar.

Delightful mask. I'd air on the side of caution for those with very oily skin, but those with dry to combination types you guys need to lap this up. After using it as a night mask the next day my skin feels plumps and my fine lines are more visibly smooth.

Ingredients

Water, Glycerin, Butylene Glycol, Tocopheryl Acetate, Niacinamide, Sodium Hyaluronate, Carrageenan, Gellan Gum, Betaine, Algin, Mannan, Ceramide NP, PEG-60 Hydrogenated Castor Oil, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Chlorphenesin, Arginine, Ethylhexylglycerin, Centella Asiatica Extract, Rubus Fruticosus (Blackberry) Fruit Extract, Salicornia Herbacea Extract, Phragmites Communis Extract, Adenosine, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil, Eucalyptus Globulus Leaf Oil, Pelargonium Graveolens Flower Oil, Citrus Limon (Lemon) Peel Oil, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis (Orange) Peel Oil, Cananga Odorata Flower Oil
